<p><p>Thermal Interface Materials (TIMs) play a crucial role in power electronics by enhancing thermal conductivity between components and heat sinks. The silicone-based TIMs offer excellent thermal performance, flexibility, and electrical insulation, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. In contrast, non-silicone TIMs, which include materials like polyimide and epoxy, are favored for their superior thermal conductivity and stability at high temperatures.
