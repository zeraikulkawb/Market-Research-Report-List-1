<p><p>The bio-based renewable jet fuel market comprises several key types. HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is derived from fats and oils, while FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is produced from biomass through gasification. ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) converts alcohols into jet fuel. Co-processing integrates bio feedstocks with conventional refining processes. Other emerging technologies continue to develop, contributing to a diversified portfolio for sustainable aviation fuel solutions.</p></p>
