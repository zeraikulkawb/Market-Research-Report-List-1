<p><ul><li>Hot Type</li><li>Quantum Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared thermal imaging systems are categorized into two main types: hot type and quantum type. Hot type systems utilize thermographic cameras that detect infrared radiation from objects to produce images based on temperature differences. They are typically used in industrial applications and diagnostics. Quantum type systems, on the other hand, leverage advanced semiconductor materials to detect lower levels of infrared radiation, offering higher sensitivity and better performance in various applications, including security and scientific research. Both types cater to specific market needs.</p></p>
