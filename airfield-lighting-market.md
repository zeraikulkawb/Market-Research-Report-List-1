<p><p>The airfield lighting market encompasses various types essential for safe airport operations. Runway lighting ensures aircraft can land and take off safely, while taxiway lighting guides them between runways and terminals. Approach lighting assists pilots in final descent, enhancing visibility during critical landing phases. Apron lighting illuminates the aircraft parking and loading areas, ensuring safety for ground operations. Each lighting type plays a crucial role in maintaining navigational safety and operational efficiency at airfields.</p></p>
