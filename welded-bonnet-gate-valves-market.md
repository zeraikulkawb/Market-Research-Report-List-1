<p><p>Welded bonnet gate valves are essential components in various industrial applications, offering robust performance and reliability. The market is segmented into two main types: threaded end and seal welded end. Threaded end valves utilize screw connections for easy installation and maintenance, making them suitable for smaller pipelines. In contrast, seal welded end valves provide enhanced leak resistance and structural integrity by welding the bonnet to the body, ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, ensuring safety and durability.</p></p>
