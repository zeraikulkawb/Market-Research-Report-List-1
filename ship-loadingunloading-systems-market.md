<p><p>The ship loading/unloading systems market encompasses two primary types: mechanical and pneumatic systems. Mechanical ship loading/unloading systems utilize equipment like conveyors and cranes for transferring bulk materials, offering strength and efficiency for heavy cargo. In contrast, pneumatic systems rely on air pressure to transport granular or powdered materials, providing a cleaner and more controlled operation. Each system caters to different cargo types and operational requirements, playing a crucial role in enhancing logistics and supply chain efficiency at ports.</p></p>
