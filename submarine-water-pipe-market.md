<p><p>The submarine water pipe market is divided into two main types: shallow water pipes and deep water pipes. Shallow water pipes are typically installed in coastal areas, where depths range from a few meters to around 50 meters, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. In contrast, deep water pipes are laid in deeper oceanic regions, often exceeding 50 meters, requiring advanced technology and specialized equipment. Both types cater to different needs, such as freshwater transport, sewage discharge, and industrial applications.</p></p>
