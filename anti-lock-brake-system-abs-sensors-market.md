<p><p>The Anti-lock Brake System (ABS) Sensors market is divided into two main types: active sensors and passive sensors. Active sensors utilize external power to generate signals, providing real-time data on wheel speed and enhancing vehicle stability during braking. In contrast, passive sensors rely on the magnetic properties of the wheel's rotation to produce signals without any external power, making them simpler and cost-effective. Both types are essential for ensuring effective ABS functionality and improving overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
