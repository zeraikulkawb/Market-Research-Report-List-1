<p><strong>The Infrared Heat Wave Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR)</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared heat wave detectors are classified into three main types based on their wavelength sensitivity. Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR) operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 µm range, ideal for applications like surveillance and agricultural monitoring. Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR), spanning 3 to 5 µm, are commonly used in thermal imaging for military and industrial applications. Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR), covering 8 to 14 µm, excel in detecting heat signatures, making them suitable for environmental monitoring and fire detection.</p></p>
