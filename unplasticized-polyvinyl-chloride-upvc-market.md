<p><p>Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride (uPVC) is a rigid, durable material widely used in construction and plumbing. The uPVC market is divided into two main types: modified uPVC and unmodified uPVC. Modified uPVC incorporates additives to enhance properties like flexibility, impact resistance, and weatherability, making it suitable for specific applications. Unmodified uPVC, on the other hand, retains its inherent rigidity and strength, making it ideal for structural applications like windows, doors, and pipes, where stability is crucial.</p></p>
