<p><ul><li>Single Track Manual Tile Cutter</li><li>Double Track Manual Tile Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual tile cutter market is categorized primarily into two types: single track and double track manual tile cutters. Single track manual tile cutters typically feature a single guiding rail, allowing for simple and efficient cutting of tiles but may limit precise handling for larger tiles. In contrast, double track manual tile cutters incorporate two guiding rails, providing enhanced stability and accuracy, making them suitable for handling larger or thicker tiles with greater ease and precision in cutting.</p></p>
