<p><p>The Metal Joining Services market encompasses various techniques for joining metals. Furnace brazing involves heating components in a controlled environment to bond them with a filler metal. Induction brazing utilizes electromagnetic fields to rapidly heat specific areas for efficient joining. Electron beam welding employs focused electron beams in a vacuum to achieve precise and strong welds. Hydrogen brazing uses a hydrogen atmosphere to enhance the brazing process, while other methods include laser welding and resistance welding, broadening the joining options.</p></p>
