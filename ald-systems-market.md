<p><strong>The ALD Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-ALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) systems are categorized primarily into Plasma-Enhanced ALD (PE-ALD) and Thermal ALD. PE-ALD utilizes plasma to enhance the deposition process, allowing for lower temperature applications and improved film quality on complex substrates. In contrast, Thermal ALD relies solely on temperature-driven chemical reactions, offering greater control over film thickness and uniformity but typically operating at higher temperatures. Both market types cater to diverse industries, including semiconductor manufacturing, where precise film characteristics are essential.</p></p>
