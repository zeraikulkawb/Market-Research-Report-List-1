<p><strong>The Mass Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quadrupole Mass Analyzer</li><li>Time of Flight Mass Analyzer</li><li>Magnetic Sector Mass Analyzer</li><li>Electrostatic Sector Mass Analyzer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass analyzers market comprises various types, each serving unique analytical needs. Quadrupole mass analyzers utilize electric fields to filter ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio, offering good sensitivity and resolution. Time of Flight (TOF) mass analyzers measure the time ions take to travel a fixed distance, allowing for high mass accuracy. Magnetic sector analyzers use magnetic fields to separate ions, known for high resolution. Electrostatic sector analyzers employ electric fields for ion separation, while “Others” includes emerging technologies like ion trap mass analyzers.</p></p>
