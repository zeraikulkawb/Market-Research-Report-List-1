<p><strong>The Remote Telemetry Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless RTU</li><li>Wired RTU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote Telemetry Units (RTU) market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize radio frequencies, cellular networks, or satellite communications to transmit data without physical connections, offering flexibility and ease of installation. They are ideal for remote locations. In contrast, wired RTUs rely on physical cabling, such as Ethernet or serial connections, ensuring stable and secure data transmission. While wired RTUs may require more infrastructure, they often provide lower latency and are suited for environments with reliable power sources.</p></p>
