<p><strong>The Hair Restoration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Extraction</li><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair restoration market primarily includes two types: Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT). FUE involves harvesting individual hair follicles from the scalp using a specialized tool, resulting in minimal scarring and a quicker recovery. In contrast, FUT involves removing a strip of hair-bearing scalp, which is then dissected into follicular units for transplant, often leaving a linear scar. Both techniques aim to restore hair density and improve hairline appearance, catering to varying patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
