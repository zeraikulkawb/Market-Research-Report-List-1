<p><p>The microfluidic connectors market primarily includes two types: circular and linear microfluidic connectors. Circular microfluidic connectors facilitate fluid transfer in a rounded configuration, enhancing compactness and ease of integration in devices. Conversely, linear microfluidic connectors enable straight fluid pathways, making them suitable for applications requiring precise flow control and alignment. Both types are crucial for various applications in biotechnology, healthcare, and analytical chemistry, driven by the demand for miniaturization and efficient fluid management in lab-on-a-chip technologies.</p></p>
