<p><p>The sorbitol powder market comprises two main types: pan-agglomerated sorbitol and spray-dried sorbitol. Pan-agglomerated sorbitol is produced through agglomeration processes that enhance density and flow, making it suitable for various applications in food and pharmaceuticals. Spray-dried sorbitol, on the other hand, is created by rapidly drying a liquid sorbitol solution, resulting in a fine powder with high solubility and excellent dispersibility, ideal for beverages and dietary supplements. Both types serve diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
