<p><p>The fixed tower crane market comprises two main types: horizontal arm type and movable arm type. The horizontal arm type features a fixed horizontal jib, providing stability and efficient lifting capacity, ideal for large construction sites. In contrast, the movable arm type allows for pivoting and flexibility in lifting operations, accommodating various angles and heights, making it suitable for diverse applications. Both types cater to specific project needs, emphasizing safety, efficiency, and adaptability in modern construction practices.</p></p>
