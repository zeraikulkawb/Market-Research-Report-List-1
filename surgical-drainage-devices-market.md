<p><strong>The Surgical Drainage Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Drainage Devices</li><li>Passive Drainage Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drainage devices market is primarily categorized into active and passive drainage devices. Active drainage devices utilize suction or negative pressure to efficiently remove fluids from surgical sites, promoting faster healing and reducing infection risk. Conversely, passive drainage devices rely on gravity or capillary action to draw out excess fluids without any mechanical assistance, making them simpler and easier to use. Each type serves specific clinical needs, ensuring appropriate fluid management in varying surgical procedures.</p></p>
