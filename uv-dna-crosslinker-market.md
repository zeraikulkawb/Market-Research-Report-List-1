<p><p>The UV DNA crosslinker market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength: shortwave UV, mediumwave UV, and longwave UV. Shortwave UV emits wavelengths around 254 nm, ideal for effective DNA crosslinking and sterilizing surfaces. Mediumwave UV, typically around 300-320 nm, offers moderate crosslinking efficiency and is often used in research applications. Longwave UV, with wavelengths above 320 nm, is less effective for DNA crosslinking but can be used in specific applications like phototherapy and surface decontamination.</p></p>
