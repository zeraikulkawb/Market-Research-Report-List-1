<p><ul><li>EEG Devices</li><li>MEG Devices</li><li>TCD Devices</li><li>ICP Monitors</li><li>Cerebral Oximeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brain monitoring devices market encompasses various technologies used to assess brain activity and health. EEG devices measure electrical activity in the brain through electrodes placed on the scalp. MEG devices detect magnetic fields produced by neuronal activity, providing high spatial resolution. TCD devices assess blood flow in cerebral arteries using ultrasound. ICP monitors measure intracranial pressure, crucial for trauma and neurological conditions. Cerebral oximeters gauge oxygen levels in the brain, aiding in stroke and brain injury management. Together, these devices enhance neurological diagnostics and patient care.</p></p>
