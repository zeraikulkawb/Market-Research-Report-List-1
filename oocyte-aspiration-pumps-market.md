<p><p>The oocyte aspiration pumps market features primarily two types: single vacuum aspiration pumps and dual vacuum aspiration pumps. Single vacuum aspiration pumps utilize a single vacuum source to create suction for oocyte retrieval during assisted reproductive procedures, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, dual vacuum aspiration pumps employ two vacuum sources for enhanced control and efficiency, allowing for more precise oocyte retrieval and reducing the risk of damage to surrounding tissues. This versatility caters to various clinical needs in fertility treatments.</p></p>
