<p><p>The Precision Laser Processing System market includes three main types: gas lasers, liquid lasers, and solid-state lasers. Gas lasers use a gas medium, offering high power and efficiency, making them ideal for cutting and engraving. Liquid lasers utilize a liquid gain medium, providing excellent beam quality and versatility, suitable for delicate applications. Solid-state lasers feature a solid gain medium, delivering high precision and energy, widely used in industrial manufacturing and medical applications. Each type caters to specific processing requirements and industries.</p></p>
